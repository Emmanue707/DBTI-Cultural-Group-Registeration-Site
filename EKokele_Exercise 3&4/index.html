What is a cultural group in DBTI?

In DBTI a cultural group is somewhat like provincial group that
each students has to fall into each provincial or cultural groups
according to where they come from and what culture they practice.

why cultural groups?

cultural groups are important because DBTI usually hosts many
events and occations which usually involve cultural/traditional
costumes (bilas) and dances and also during mess cultural groups
lead and do community services as well.
"You may be far from your home province but with your own
cultural group, you feel comfortable and feel at
home( isn't that right?) Hmm!! that is why cultural group matters."
